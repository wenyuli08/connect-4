Connect Four is a 2-player game in which the players take turns dropping one colored disc from the top into a seven-column, six-row vertically suspended grid.

Whoever is the first to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of four of one's own discs wins!

For this version, you will assume that only one user is viewing the page that presents your game at a time.
